About 关于

MagicAWRT

Compability 相容性

MagicAWRT is compatible with Series 0 - 6 which makes it very efficient in usage. You don't need to buy an adapter for every model. This make MagicAWRT unique!

MagicAWRT与0-6系列兼容，这使得它在使用上非常有效。你不需要为每个模型购买一个适配器。这使得MagicAWRT独一无二!

Quality 质量

MagicAWRT is made of the best materials and is working 100% with your watches. No connectivity issues like with other bad-quality adapters from competitors.

MagicAWRT是由最好的材料制成的，并且100%与您的手表一起工作。没有像其他竞争对手的劣质适配器那样的连接问题。

Easy to use 易于使用

All you need to do is opening the diagnostic port on your Watch  and connect the watch with  MagicAWRT to your mac.
Then you can restore the firmware!

你需要做的就是打开你的手表上的诊断端口，用MagicAWRT将手表连接到你的Mac上。然后你就可以恢复固件了!

UART Support 
UART支持

MagicAWRT supports the UART Serial Protocol to communicate with your Watches. 
This functionality is useful for debugging and crash logs, but also for a smooth interaction in Purple Mode.
A SPAM2 cable is required...

MagicAWRT支持UART串行协议来与你的手表通信。
这个功能对于调试和崩溃日志非常有用，而且在紫色模式下也能实现流畅的互动。
需要一条SPAM2电缆...

Software Support 
软件支持

With the purchase of a MagicAWRT  you are authorized for the firmware iPSW download and
can use MagicClock.
Solve white Screen, “!" point, Restart error, Screen Show Error, Touch No, others faults...

购买了MagicAWRT后，您就获得了下载iPSW固件的授权，可以使用MagicClock。
可以使用MagicClock。
解决白屏，"！"点，重启错误，屏幕显示错误，触摸不，其他故障...

Help environment
帮助环境

When using MagicAWRT to repair, restore or revive an Apple Watch, you are helping the environment and reduce the global amount of e-waste.We are supporters of the #RightToRepair debate!
Reuse or repair instead of throw away and buy new!

当使用MagicAWRT维修、修复或恢复Apple Watch时，您正在帮助环境，减少全球电子垃圾的数量。我们是#RightToRepair辩论的支持者!
重复使用或维修，而不是扔掉和购买新的
